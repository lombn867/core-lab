ARONY N
NORA LOMBARDO | NEW YORK, NY
Nora Lombardo is a designer, writer, and photographer based in New York City. Born and raised in what is dubbed the "sixth burough," Yonkers is where Arony N came to fruition. Arony N describes the artist behind mixed-media works commenting on themes of objectification, youth, mental health, and identity. Belonging to the Communication Design Department for Parsons at The New School, the young artist hopes to compose a series of works that lend themselves to further developing and understanding her identity in the design world.
